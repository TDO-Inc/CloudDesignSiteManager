# Website Project Templates

## Milestones (shared by both templates)

Based on the current Website SOP, the portal tracks these stages:

| # | Milestone | Description | Key Actions |
|---|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Kickoff** | Contract won, initial setup complete | Kickoff call scheduled/completed, domain request form submitted, content collection begins |
| 2 | **Content Collection** | Office provides photos, copy, and brand assets | Content briefs filled out, photos uploaded, logo/branding submitted |
| 3 | **Round 1 Review** | Full site built, presented to office | Staff reviews with office, collects feedback, identifies missing content |
| 4 | **Revisions** | Changes applied based on Round 1 feedback | Round 2 review if needed, firm launch date set |
| 5 | **Pre-Launch Setup** | Technical configuration and compliance | SEO setup (RankMath configuration), ADA compliance review, Google Analytics install, Hotjar install, privacy policy added |
| 6 | **Training & Approval** | Office trained and gives written go-ahead | Training call (editing with Beaver Builder, analytics overview, SEO basics), written launch approval received from doctor/project contact |
| 7 | **Launch** | Site goes live | Domain pointed, developer pushes live, "Site Is Now Live" email sent, 6-month follow-up task created |

**Note:** Some actions happen outside the portal (billing tickets, HubSpot deal updates, monday.com assignments). The portal tracks the milestones and handles the content collection — it doesn't replace the full SOP workflow in V1. Integration with HubSpot and monday.com comes in Phase 2.

## File categories (shared by both templates)

| Category | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Logo & Branding** | Office logo files, brand colors, fonts | Logo (PNG, SVG, AI), brand guidelines |
| **Doctor Photos** | Individual headshots of each doctor | Portrait orientation, professional |
| **Staff Photos** | Individual and group staff photos | Portrait orientation for individual, landscape for group |
| **Office Photos - Exterior** | Outside of office | So patients know what the building looks like |
| **Office Photos - Waiting Room** | Patient waiting area | Empty AND with doctor/staff greeting patient |
| **Office Photos - Front Desk** | Front desk area | Empty AND with smiling front office staff |
| **Office Photos - Hallways** | Hallway/corridor shots | Used as background images on site |
| **Office Photos - Operatory** | Treatment room photos | Empty operatory, doctor at microscope, in-treatment photo |
| **Equipment Photos** | Close-ups of technology/equipment | CBCT unit, ASI Cart, microscope, GentleWave, etc. |
| **Other Photos** | Miscellaneous images | Community involvement, office events, etc. |
| **Documents** | Supporting documents | Insurance list, fee schedules, post-op instructions, privacy policy |

**Photo guidelines (shown to clients in the portal):**
- If taking photos with an iPhone, turn off Live mode
- For non-staff photos, take both landscape AND portrait orientation
- Photos should be well-lit and high resolution

## Page catalog

These are the pages available for selection. Each page has associated content brief fields.

### Core pages (included by default)

| Page | Content brief fields |
|------|---------------------|
| **Home** | Practice tagline/headline, welcome message, key differentiators (3-4 bullet points), preferred call-to-action |
| **Contact** | Office address, phone, fax, email, office hours (per day), map/directions notes, after-hours emergency info, contact form preferences |

### About section

| Page | Content brief fields |
|------|---------------------|
| **About Our Office** | Office history/story, mission statement, what makes us different, year established, community involvement |
| **Meet the Doctor(s)** | Per doctor: full name, credentials/degrees, bio, education, professional memberships, specialties, personal interests, headshot |
| **Meet the Team** | Per team member: name, role/title, bio (optional), photo |
| **Office Tour** | Descriptions/captions for office areas (optional — photos do most of the work here) |

### Patient Information section

| Page | Content brief fields |
|------|---------------------|
| **Financial Policies / Fees** | Payment methods accepted, financing options, fee schedule notes |
| **Insurance Information** | List of accepted insurance plans, out-of-network policy, insurance FAQ |
| **What to Expect Your First Visit** | Step-by-step first visit description, what to bring, estimated visit duration, any pre-visit instructions |
| **Patient Comfort Menu** | Comfort options offered (noise-canceling headphones, blankets, sedation options, etc.) |
| **Post Treatment Instructions** | Post-op care instructions by procedure type, emergency contact info |

### Endodontics (services) section

Standard services (boilerplate text available):

| Page | Content brief fields |
|------|---------------------|
| **Root Canal Treatment** | Custom description (or use boilerplate), FAQ, any unique approach notes |
| **Root Canal Retreatment** | Custom description (or use boilerplate), when retreatment is needed |
| **Apicoectomy** | Custom description (or use boilerplate), what to expect |
| **Cracked Teeth** | Custom description (or use boilerplate), types of cracks, treatment options |

Offices can add additional service pages beyond the standard four. Custom services get a title + free-form description field (no boilerplate available).

### Technology section

Selectable from a predefined list. Each selected technology gets a page or section.

| Technology option | Content brief fields |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Digital Radiography** | Custom description (or use boilerplate), benefits to patients |
| **CBCT (Cone Beam CT)** | Custom description (or use boilerplate), benefits to patients |
| **Surgical Operating Microscope** | Custom description (or use boilerplate) |
| **TDO Software** | Custom description (or use boilerplate) |
| **GentleWave** | Custom description (or use boilerplate) |
| **EdgePro Laser** | Custom description (or use boilerplate) |
| *Custom technology* | Office can add unlisted technology with name + description |

### Other pages

| Page | Content brief fields |
|------|---------------------|
| **Testimonials / Reviews** | Links to Google/Yelp review pages, selected testimonial quotes (with patient permission), review platform preferences |
| *Custom page* | Office can request additional pages with a title + description of what they want |

## Template: Basic

**Description:** Office stays close to the standard template. Uses TDO boilerplate text and stock imagery for most pages. Office provides only essential custom content.

**Default pages:** Home, About Our Office, Meet the Doctor(s), Root Canal Treatment, Technology (select from list), Contact

**Content briefs collect:**
- Office name, address, phone, fax, email, hours
- Logo files
- Doctor name(s), credentials, headshot(s), short bio
- Staff group photo (optional)
- Office exterior photo
- Technology selections (checkboxes from predefined list)
- Any specific messaging preferences or "must include" items

**Everything else:** Uses boilerplate text and stock images. Staff can customize further during the Build phase without needing additional client input.

---

## Template: Standard

**Description:** Fully customized site. Office selects which pages they want and provides custom content for each. Full photo shoot recommended.

**Default pages:** All core + About section + Patient Information + Endodontics + Technology + Testimonials + Contact (office can deselect any non-core page)

**Content briefs collect:**
- Full content brief for every selected page (see fields above)
- All photo categories (full recommended photo list)
- Doctor bios, credentials, education, memberships
- Individual staff photos and bios
- Office-specific descriptions for services and technology
- Insurance list, fee information, comfort menu items
- Custom page requests

---

## How page selection works in the portal

1. During project creation, staff selects the template (Basic or Standard)
2. The template pre-selects default pages
3. Staff (or client) can add/remove pages from the catalog
4. For Technology pages, a checkbox list of available technologies is shown
5. Content briefs are generated only for the selected pages
6. Custom pages can be added with a title and free-form description field
